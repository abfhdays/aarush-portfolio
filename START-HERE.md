## To Use This:

1. **Copy these files** to your project (replace existing ones)
2. **Run** `git add .` and `git commit -m "baseline redesign"`
3. **Customize** the Header with your info
4. **Add** your actual work and projects to page.tsx
5. **Tweak** colors in globals.css to match your vibe

## Key Differences from Your Old Site

| Old | New |
|-----|-----|

| Badges/chips everywhere | Plain text |
| Serif headings | System fonts |
